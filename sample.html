Meet the Gang

Brothers Cuphead and Mugman are joined by the clever, adventurous Ms. Chalice for a rollicking adventure on a previously undiscovered Inkwell Isle!

Cuphead

Cuphead is the titular main protagonist of Cuphead. He is Mugman's irresponsible older twin brother and lives on Inkwell Isle.

Mugman

Mugman is the deuteragonist of Cuphead. He is Cuphead's sensible younger twin brother and, like the rest of his family, lives on Inkwell Isle.

Ms.Chalice

Ms. Chalice is the tritagonist of Cuphead. She was first revealed in the upcoming DLC, Cuphead: The Delicious Last Course.

About the Game

Cuphead the Game

Cuphead is a classic run and gun action game heavily focused on boss battles. Inspired by cartoons of the 1930s, the visuals and audio are painstakingly created with the same techniques of the era, i.e. traditional hand drawn cel animation, watercolor backgrounds, and original jazz recordings.
